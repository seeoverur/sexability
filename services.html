Pathways to Intimacy

We offer a range of confidential, professional services designed to support you on your unique journey toward a more fulfilling intimate life.

Personalized 1-on-1 Coaching

Our confidential coaching sessions provide dedicated support tailored to your personal journey. We can explore a range of topics together, including:

  • Dating with a disability
  • Improving self-esteem & body image
  • Exploring sexual identity & expression
  • Discovering self-pleasure & pleasure with others
  • Managing communication with partners
  • Maintaining a healthy sex life
  • Navigating physical assistance during sex
  • Adaptive sexual aids and tools
  • Guidance on family planning

Sessions are flexibly offered in-person, by phone, or via Skype.

Community Support Group

Join our private Facebook group to connect with peers who understand. It's a safe space to share experiences, learn new skills, build confidence, and feel the power of community. Our group is open to individuals with any physical disability and welcomes all forms of sexual expression.

Partner Surrogacy Program

For some clients, working with a surrogate partner is a therapeutic step. A surrogate is a trained professional who helps clients achieve intimacy goals through guided, hands-on experiences. This program is designed to build confidence and improve future sexual relationships in a safe, structured, and goal-oriented environment.
